Within the Excitement of the Circus Tent

A seven-ring circus occupies the gigantic tent you have entered. The performers are rehearsing for their show that night. In one of the side rings a gigantic dragon with a really buff human arm practices incinerating thatched-roof cottages. In another ring you see a man setting climbing into a large cannon. But it is the center ring that really catches your attention. There you see the impossible happening. A magnetic monopole is being created right before your very eyes!
