Training with Trogdor the Burninator

As you approach the dragon, you see a sign that says 'Trogdor the Burninator-peasants beware!' Despite that intimidating sign, you continue forward to go ask the dragon-keeper for a job. Right before you can get your first word out, Trogdor decides that you look like a peasant and burninates you to a crisp before you can say 'whoa!'
